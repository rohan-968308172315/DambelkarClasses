Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses

Our courses have different durations:

  • Beginner Level: 3 months (36 sessions)
  • Intermediate Level: 4 months (48 sessions)
  • Advanced Level: 6 months (72 sessions)
  • Workshops: 2-4 weeks depending on the program

Each session is 90 minutes long.

We offer multiple batches throughout the day to accommodate different schedules:

  • Morning: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Afternoon: 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM
  • Evening: 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM
  • Weekend: Special batches on Saturday & Sunday

New batches start every month. You can choose a batch that suits your schedule after an initial assessment.

We welcome students of all ages! Currently, our youngest student is 12 years old and our oldest is 65. We have different teaching approaches for different age groups:

  • Teenagers (12-18): More activity-based learning
  • Adults (19-50): Career-focused communication
  • Seniors (50+): Practical daily usage focus

The only requirement is the willingness to learn and practice.

We offer flexible payment options:

  • Full payment (5% discount)
  • Two installments
  • Monthly installments (for longer courses)

We accept:

  • Cash
  • UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, PayTM)
  • Bank Transfer
  • Credit/Debit Cards (extra 2% processing fee)

Yes, we provide a certificate of completion for all our regular courses (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced) after:

  • Attending minimum 80% of classes
  • Clearing the final assessment

Additionally, we offer recommendation letters for exceptional students applying for jobs or higher education.
